## Content Format Matrix

### X (Twitter)
- **Best formats:** Short takes (< 280 chars), threads (5-10 tweets), quote tweets with commentary
- **Tone:** Direct, punchy, opinionated. Like talking to a smart friend at a bar.
- **Visuals:** Optional but screenshots/diagrams boost engagement
- **Timing:** 8h-9h and 12h-13h (French business hours)

### Instagram
- **Best formats:** Carousels (5-8 slides, educational), Reels (15-30s, demos), Stories (polls, Q&A)
- **Tone:** Same directness but slightly more polished visuals
- **Visuals:** Mandatory. Consistent template with brand colors.
- **Timing:** 12h-13h and 18h-19h

### TikTok
- **Best formats:** Short video (15-60s), screen recordings of AI in action, talking head + demo
- **Tone:** Casual, fast-paced, no corporate filter
- **Visuals:** Raw/authentic > polished. Trending sounds when relevant.
- **Timing:** 18h-21h
